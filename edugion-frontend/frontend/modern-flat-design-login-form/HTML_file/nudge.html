The Extra Push for the Great Effort

As Edugion consists of many job seekers, we understand the effort to create a profile for the perfect fit on your dream companies. We also understand the sadness of getting rejected from them without having your profile getting checked at all. With these problems in mind, Edugion comes up with the connection between us and other companies to provide a feature called "nudge". This feature will ensure that your profile and interaction of you and your dream company will be viewed by the companies.

Why is it important?

For the job seekers, this feature will ensure you that your application, videos, and engagements on other media related to your companies will not be neglected. With our nudge, the chance of continuing into the next process will be higher

For companies, since people committing to the nudge has a cost to make, this feature will enhance the candidates with high engagement and dedication to show the desire to join the companies. This feature could als help companies create a loyal, dedicated group of workers to work for, which is important to a successful organization

Why us? Not others?

Despite not being an absolute unique feature, our "nudge" is superior compared to other job portal with the similar idea.
