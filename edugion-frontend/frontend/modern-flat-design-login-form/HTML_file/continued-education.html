Edugion and the importance of media that we see

As Edugion desires to create a job site that everyone could pursue their dream companies, we view that enhancing candidate's skills, both soft skills and hard skills, is an important function to make this goal more intact. Although several education channels exist in many job portal by now, most of them focus only courses to provide the education for candidates, while in fact other channels such as news or events are equally important. This leads to us to combine several channel together in one site
