Questions that defines you better

Our mission is to create a job site that gives a chance for job seeker to express themselves further than just education and work experience. Apart from a personal video that allow a self-expression beyond the work, we achieve this by forming a questionnaire that could pull your abilities within a few questions.

Created to cover a wide range of skills

We started with research on the important areas for professional improvement at work from several resources -- professional consult and website included. We then aggregate areas that are unanimously suggested by most resources. Some topics could be seen on the left side. Then, our subject-matter experts develop the questions specifically for each skill, and peer-reviewed by other experts to ensure its effectiveness. Our questionnaire contains both closed-ended and open-ended question for flexibility of users.

Instruction for answering the question

  1. Go to the profile page
  2. Click on the "Questionnaire Portion"
  3. After the questionnaire open, you can answer 1-2 question for each category to show your skills and experience
  4. If you want to answer more than that, click add question which will lead you to the product page to pay for answering more
